What We Do

At Bhooswarga, we integrate practice, learning, and innovation through:

Process 1: Preparation and planning

Process 2: Soil and planting setup

Process 3: Growth monitoring and care

Process 4: Harvest and delivery

Process Video

Watch how our fertilizer and growing process works on the Bhooswarga farm.

Sustainable Urban Farming

  • Organic cultivation using climate-resilient practices
  • Multiple crop cycles including vegetables, greens, fruits, and herbs
  • Drip irrigation for efficient water use
  • Composting of organic waste
  • Natural pest management using neem-based solutions

Living Lab for Learning & Research

  • Hands-on learning for Civil Engineering, Sustainable Engineering, Environmental Studies, and allied disciplines
  • Live projects on soil improvement, water management, irrigation systems, waste-to-resource models, and green infrastructure
  • Platform for interdisciplinary R&D, innovation pilots, and student-led experimentation

Community & Student Engagement

  • Daily student volunteering, including weekends and semester breaks
  • Internships, live projects, and project-based learning
  • Development of life skills: teamwork, responsibility, leadership, and problem-solving

Well-Being & Inclusion

  • Calm green spaces supporting mental well-being
  • Nature-based learning and reflection zones
