About Bhooswarga

Bhooswarga – Heaven on Earth

Bhooswarga (meaning Heaven on Earth) is an interdisciplinary urban sustainability initiative rooted in the belief that healthy soil, healthy food, and healthy communities are deeply connected.

Started as a small campus pilot at Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bengaluru, Bhooswarga has grown into a vibrant living laboratory where students, faculty, and volunteers come together to practice sustainable farming, climate-resilient design, and community-centric innovation.

What began as a garden is now evolving into a city-scale model for urban farming, education, and climate action.

Our Team

Dr. Sumaraj

Assistant Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, NMIT

Dr. Sumaraj is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology, Bengaluru. She holds an M.Tech from IIT Kharagpur and a PhD from the University of Auckland, New Zealand. An expert in Environmental Engineering and Sustainable Technologies, her work focuses on translating research into real-world impact through experiential learning and community-driven initiatives. As the founder and project lead of Bhooswarga, she integrates urban farming, climate resilience, and engineering education to build inclusive, hands-on learning spaces that promote sustainability, well-being, and innovation.
